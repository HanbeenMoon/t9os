# ADR-044: Gemini Model Restriction

- Date: 2026-03-10
- Status: Accepted
- Decision: Gemini 2.x models are permanently banned. Only gemini-3-flash or gemini-3.1-pro are allowed.
- Rationale: Gemini 2.x produced unreliable outputs in guardian reviews and bulk processing. Model quality directly affects guardian judgment quality.
- Outcome: L1 hard rule, enforced in `gm` alias

## Simondon Mapping
Quality of the associated milieu — unreliable tools degrade the environment in which individuation occurs.
